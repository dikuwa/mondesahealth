# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mondesa Health serves two closely connected groups. Patients and families in Mondesa and greater Swakopmund use the public site, often on mobile devices, to understand the practice, book appointments, and manage booking details without creating an account. Practice owners and authorised staff use the dashboard to manage appointments, patients, claims, finance, availability, settings, staff access, and operational activity.

## Product Purpose

Provide a trustworthy public front door and a dependable medical-practice workspace. The public experience should make care easy to understand and appointments easy to arrange. The authenticated experience should help staff complete daily administrative work accurately, quickly, and securely. Success means patients can act without confusion and staff can manage the practice without layout friction or hidden information on any common screen size.

## Brand Personality

Calm, attentive, and dependable. The experience should feel professional without becoming institutional, contemporary without chasing trends, and grounded in the local community. Copy should be plain, respectful, reassuring, and concise.

## Anti-references

Avoid sterile corporate healthcare templates, bright neon palettes, generic technology gradients, decorative motion, low-contrast interfaces, dense desktop-only layouts, novelty controls, and visual effects that compete with patient care or operational tasks. Do not rely on hover, colour alone, or hidden desktop conventions for essential actions.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next action unmistakable.
2. Preserve context and critical information across every viewport.
3. Earn trust through clarity, consistency, and visible system state.
4. Keep patient-facing language human and staff workflows efficient.
5. Design for real mobile use first, including touch, browser chrome, virtual keyboards, and slower devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain at least 4.5:1 text contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic landmarks and labels, screen-reader-friendly status messaging, reduced-motion support, and touch targets of at least 44 by 44 pixels. Never make hover the only way to discover or operate functionality. Support narrow screens, zoom, coarse pointers, safe-area insets, long names and labels, and clear validation and error recovery.
